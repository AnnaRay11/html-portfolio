Best Series To Watch According to Anna

My top 3

Dark

A German science fiction thriller tv series for those who love time travel and complex stories with their twists.

Raised by Wolves

An American science fiction drama tv series about two androids with a mission to raise human children on the
mysterious planet Kepler-22b after the Earth was destroyed by a religious war.

The Last Kingdom

A British historical drama tv series based on historical events and people of the Viking Age.
